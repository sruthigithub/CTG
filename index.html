Who We Are

Catalyst Technical Group is a ONE STOP SHOP for Project Planning, Permitting & Approvals from Concept to Completion. CTG offers many advantages in the execution of work on your project, offering expertise in Planning, Urban Design, Parks & Recreation Planning & Design, Landscape Architecture, Engineering and Construction Administration under one roof.

We provide the client with a knowledge-based company to complete a project in a functional and creative manner. We focus on solid design and sound planning with the needs and desires of our clients as our main priority. CTG provides the industry experience, commitment and proven results your project needs to help see it through completion. As a multi-disciplined construction engineering consulting firm, we provide the innovative solutions and support you need to deliver your project on time and within budget.

With a history of providing professional engineering and planning services, CTG continues its focus and dedication to provide high quality infrastructure design services for its clients. We have an established reputation for technically excellent, highly responsive, team-oriented engineering services. Maintaining a focus on the goals of our clients has allowed us to be recognized as dedicated and valued members of the project design team.
